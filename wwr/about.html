History

G-Linds Rafting was founded from a passion for adventure and the outdoors. What started as a love for exploring rivers grew into a mission to provide safe, exciting, and memorable rafting experiences for everyone. With a strong focus on teamwork, safety, and respect for nature, G-Linds continues to help guests enjoy the thrill of the river while creating lasting memories.
